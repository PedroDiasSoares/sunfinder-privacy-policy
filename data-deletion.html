SunFinder Data Deletion

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This page explains how users of the SunFinder mobile app can request deletion of data associated with SunFinder. SunFinder is provided by Xago.

Request Deletion

To request deletion of data associated with SunFinder, email us at sunfinder.appsupport@gmail.com with the subject line "SunFinder data deletion request".

Please include enough information for us to identify the request, such as the email address you previously used to contact SunFinder support. Do not send passwords, payment card numbers, or sensitive documents.

Data Deleted

If you contacted SunFinder by email, we can delete support correspondence and related contact details that we control, unless we are required to keep them for legal, security, or abuse-prevention reasons.

Data Stored On Your Device

SunFinder stores preferences and cached weather/search data locally on your device. You can delete this local data at any time by clearing SunFinder's app storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Data We Do Not Control

Purchases are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store payment systems. External booking websites, advertising providers, weather providers, and app stores may keep their own records under their own privacy policies. To delete data held by those services, use their own privacy or account tools.

Retention Period

We aim to respond to deletion requests within 30 days. Some limited records may be kept for longer if needed for legal, tax, security, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes.

Privacy Policy

For more information, see the SunFinder Privacy Policy.
